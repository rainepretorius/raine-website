Solutions Architecture

Smart home system design, integration & technical strategy.

All Skills

Solutions Architecture is at the heart of what I do at Untamed Technologies. I design smart home systems that bring together hardware, software, and automation into seamless, reliable experiences — translating what a homeowner or developer actually wants into a technical solution that just works.

Smart home architecture is about making complex integrations feel simple. I work across the full stack — device protocols, local and remote control systems, automation logic, and the interfaces people interact with every day. Getting the architecture right means the system is easy to extend, bulletproof in daily use, and intuitive to live with.

What I can do

  • Smart home system design and integration planning
  • Device and protocol integration (Z-Wave, Zigbee, Wi-Fi, Matter)
  • Home automation logic and scene design
  • Local and remote control architecture
  • Home Assistant integration and custom component development
  • Technical strategy and solution scoping for smart home projects
